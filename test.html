The wonderful life in Christ

Takeaways from the gospel of John

A joyful life

River of living waters will flow in your heart. You will fill satisfied.

A fearless life

You will not be afraid. You are saved and have eternal life. Death cannot harm you.

A bright life

Jesus is light and will not walk in darkness. Your path will be bright as day. He will guide you.
